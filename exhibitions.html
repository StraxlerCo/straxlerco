Pasean Dusk

Landscape

I found this scene after a long day of exploring. These two fishermen, having just returned from the sea, were preparing their nets for the next trip. The stunning sunset was the perfect backdrop for their quiet dedication. For me, this image captures the beauty not just of the landscape, but of the daily life and unwavering spirit of the people who live it.

Blue Hour Café

Landscape

After a long journey away from home, I made a quick stop in Tanjung Bumi, Madura. The beachside cafe was bathed in the serenity of the blue hour, and the Morning Star shone brightly, welcoming my arrival. This photo is a sweet memory of that brief pause—a moment of peace before finally reuniting with my family at home.
